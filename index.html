Ref
Payment
Ngam Cafe is cash only. Most street food stalls prefer cash. Bring VND 200,000–500,000 daily.
Timing
Arrive at Pho Quynh before 8AM to avoid queues. Eggyolk gets crowded after 10AM.
Weather
Hot and humid. Do outdoor spots (Book Street, rooftop) in the morning. Malls in the afternoon.
Day 1 bus
Kumho Samco departs 06:30 PP. Border crossing can add 30–60 min. Expect arrival ~15:00–16:00.
Transport
Days 2 & 3 spots are within District 1 — walkable or a short Grab ride. Day 3 wholesale market may need Grab.
Coffee order
At Cong Ca Phe: order cà phê dừa (coconut coffee). At Eggyolk: try the Bailey's Yolk.
Coffee Apt tip
Take the stairs — elevator charges ~30,000 VND. Stairs let you browse every floor.
